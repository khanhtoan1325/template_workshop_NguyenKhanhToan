During my internship and participation in learning activities with the AWS community, I had the opportunity to attend two events related to **Cloud Computing**, **Generative AI**, **AI Agents**, **DevOps**, **Amazon Q**, and system security on AWS. These events helped me expand my practical knowledge, better understand current technology trends, and gain clearer direction for my learning process and career development.

The events featured many speakers, engineers, Solution Architects, and members of the AWS community. Through the sharing sessions and practical demos, I learned how AWS services are applied in enterprises, how AI supports system operations, how workflows can be automated, and how security effectiveness can be improved.
